Frequently Asked Questions



What are your store hours?


Store Hours are: Monday - Thursday : 9:00 - 9:00, Saturday 9:00 - 10:00, and closed on Sunday.

Yes! We are happy to take orders for our customers, just come in and request an order form from the cashier

Unfortunately highly anticipated New Releases are on a firt come first serve basis. However if there are any other books you would like held we would happily do so, just give us a call!
