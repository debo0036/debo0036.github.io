About Us


Fully Booked was created by Mr. and Mrs. de Boer, they created the bookstore because they wanted to share their love of reading with the community. Fully Booked has always been more than just a store, it is a place for booklovers to gather and discuss. As well Fully Booked has always played a large role in the community in order to help encourage literacy to young children.

Our Values



Fully Booked values its customers before anything else. Good customer service and ensuring our customers have the best experience possible is our number one goal.


Encouring young children to read. Fully Booked staff volunteers at local libraries to help with children programs as well as running some of our own to encourage literacy at a young age.
